Legislative Tracker

The Legislative Tracker takes final vote results for bills passed by both chambers of the Colorado General Assembly and links an individual legislator's vote to a graphical presentation of overall vote results and votes by party. Created using React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Firebase to deploy the frontend, Heroku to deploy the backend (Heroku will take few seconds), and Chart.js. Puppeteer was used to scrape the General Assembly's website for data.
