My background is in public policy and nonprofit media. I’ve always been curious and eager to learn new things, so pursuing my interest in software development felt like a natural next step. I’m currently a student in Galvanize’s Web Development Immersive program, and in the coming months, hope to use my coding and analytical skills to create new things and to find ways to use technology to serve communities better.

As a former Chief of Staff to Colorado State Senator John Kefalas, and as one of the founding staffers of the public radio debate series and podcast, Intelligence Squared U.S., I have years of experience in research, writing, running live events, and constituent services. I’ve learned to prioritize, break down complex topics, juggle multiple projects, and the importance of teamwork and collaboration.

In my spare time, I can be found hiking, rock climbing, and experimenting with high altitude baking.
